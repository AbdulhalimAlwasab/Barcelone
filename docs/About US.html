About Us

FC Barcelona (in full: Futbol Club Barcelona) is a Spanish football club located in the city of Barcelona in the Catalonia region of Spain. The club was founded on November 29, 1899 and is one of the oldest and most famous clubs in the world. Barcelona is considered one of the successful and prestigious clubs, having achieved many titles and achievements throughout history. The club is famous for its beautiful and attacking playing style, and has a wide and popular fan base around the world. Barcelona has shined in recent years, winning a large number of championships, including the Spanish League (LaLiga), the Spanish King's Cup (Copa del Rey), the European Champions League (UEFA Champions League), the Club World Cup, and others. Barcelona competes in official matches in its distinctive Catalan club uniform, which consists of a red and blue vertically striped shirt, as well as blue shorts and red socks.
