FC-Barcelona

Barcelona is a Spanish football club located in the city of Barcelona in the Catalonia region. FC Barcelona is considered one of the top clubs in world football and has a large and widespread following around the world. FC Barcelona was founded in 1899 and is one of the prominent Spanish La Liga clubs.

Camp Nou Stadium

Camp Nou Stadium is the main football stadium of FC Barcelona and is located in the city of Barcelona in the Catalonia region of Spain. Camp Nou is considered one of the largest stadiums in the world and can accommodate more than 99,000 spectators, making it one of the city's most prominent tourist attractions. Camp Nou stadium was opened on September 24, 1957 and has been renovated and expanded several times throughout history.
